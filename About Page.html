We're GiveWheel,

THE UK'S MOST CUSTOMISABLE FUNDRAISING PLATFORM.

We believe that everybody has a personal responsibility to make a positive difference and, as a result, donating and fundraising should be simple, flexible and seamless.

Founded in 2021, GiveWheel provides the vehicle for truly customisable fundraising for individuals, teams, and companies. We're a small, London-based team building an industry-leading platform, available for free with 0% platform fees. Each year, we process millions of pounds worth of donations to national and international charities from thousands of individuals, non-profits and companies using GiveWheel to fuel their fundraising.

Much of GiveWheel's functionality has come from requests for functionality - ranging from small tweaks to full product builds. The fundraising world is wide and varied - but no matter your event, we want to support you! If you think there's something missing in our platform - get in touch! We'd love to hear from you and find more ways of raising funds for great causes.

One-off, team and ticketed fundraising for individuals, non-profits and businesses. Free to all, with 0% platform fees.

At GiveWheel, we believe that everybody has a personal responsibility to make a positive difference in their community. We believe that donating and fundraising should be simple, flexible, immersive and seamless in experience. We believe that contributors should be close to the causes they care about.

Our Services

GiveWheel was developed in response to challenges that we experienced within the charitable fundraising space. Fundraising felt transactional, there was little transparency, often high compulsory deductions were taken. From the ground up, GiveWheel is designed as a transparent, integrated platform for individuals, charities and companies alike.
